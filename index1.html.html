About Food Savage

Food Savage is a platform that connects food donors with orphanages and shelters to reduce food waste and fight hunger in our communities.

How It Works

1. Donate

Restaurants, caterers, and individuals can donate excess food.

2. Verify

Our team verifies the food quality and safety.

3. Distribute

Food is distributed to orphanages and shelters in need.
